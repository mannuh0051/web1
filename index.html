How to Check Results

  • Step 1: Click "Check Results"

    Click the blue "Check Results" button in the top right corner of this page.

  • Step 2: Enter Your Details

    Enter your full name and index number in the simple form that appears.

  • Step 3: Get USSD Code

    You will receive a USSD code (*811*842#) to dial on your mobile phone for verification.

  • Step 4: Dial & Verify

    Dial *811*842# on your phone to complete verification and access results.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What do I need to check my results?

    You only need your full name and index number. The system will guide you through the simple verification process.

  • Why do I need to dial *811*842#?

    USSD verification ensures the security of your results and confirms your identity using your mobile phone.

  • Is the USSD service available 24/7?

    Yes, the USSD verification service (*811*842#) is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week on all mobile networks.

  • Is my information secure?

    Yes, your personal information is protected and only used for verification purposes. We comply with all data protection regulations.
